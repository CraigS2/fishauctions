Label Printing


You most likely do not need to change these settings!
  1. Print your labels on regular paper
  2. Cut them out with scissors
  3. Use packing tape to attach them to your lots (make sure your bags are dry!)

If you want to get fancy, the default settings are already set below so you can print on Avery 5160 labels

Changing any of these settings may well break something -- if you have a problem, just leave a comment here and I'll fix it.
